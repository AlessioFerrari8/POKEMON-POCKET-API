Manage your Pokémon TCG Pocket collection

Track cards, expansions, decks and missing cards in one place.

What can you do?

Search for cards

Browse and manage all Pokémon TCG Pocket cards.
Add cards you don't possess to missing-cards for finding, trading, and sharing them.

Make new friends

Connect with other collectors in the community.
Share id, trade cards with agreement, chat with others.

Build decks

Create powerful decks and discover trending strategies.
Share your own decks, rate them, and advise other users.

Trade cards

Exchange cards with other players to complete your collection.

Discover cards

Search for new cards

In the page 'cards' you can search for pokémons. All of the pokémons are from the game Pokémon Pocket TGCP. You will find each reference for the name of the pokémon you searched.

Discover cards details

Discover the id of the card and the expansion.
You can also find the HP, the Type and the Stage; additionaly there are the credits of the illustrator.

Add them in your collection

By adding cards to your collection, you can make a set of the cards you do not posses. This will help you to complete the game.

Manage missing cards

Manage all missing cards

In the page 'cards' you can search for pokémons. Here you can access all your missing cards, remove them, remove every one of them.

Add missing cards

There's also a search bar.
With it you can search cards, add them to your missing cards for finding them later.

Filter reasearch

You can also perform a filtered reasearch. You can filter for each expansion.
